PROFILE

Mr G. Yoganand is a first-generation Techno-preneur with rich experience and pioneering projects in real estate sector for more than 3 decades. Now, he is transforming himself into first generation public servant by plunging into electoral politics of 2018.


G. Yoganand , the business and real estate tycoon, a Member of the Building Approval Committee of the Municipal Corporation Hyderabad, the erstwhile Greater Hyderabad Municipal Corporation (GHMC). This was the foundation years of growth Hyderabad as we now know. He started his career during 1983-84 as a Structural Engineer and promoted M/s Matrix Design Engineers, a Civil Engineering Consultancy Firm in 1985.


The Promoter of Manjeera Group, G. Yoganand is a pioneer in adopting new systems and process in infrastructure sector. Being in execution of high rise buildings, he has introduced Building Information Modeling (BIM), Diversified Building Construction, Green Initiatives, Sustainability. Yoganand was first to establish Sewerage Treatment Plant (STP) in a Commercial Complex and was member of the initial group of activists to promote Rain Water Harvesting Schemes in Hyderabad and Energy Savings Systems in Buildings.
